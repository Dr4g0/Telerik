::GAMEPLAY::
~~~~~~~~~~~~

To move use the arrow keys - up, down, left and right for the desired direction.There are 4 ghosts that will try to stop you - avoid them and collect all the gold as quickly as possible. You got 5 lives. Everytime a ghost catches you, you will lose one life. When all lives are lost, your game is over. If you succeed to get all the gold and your timing is good, you will enter the Hall of fame. There is a special symbol - the cherry (red "@"). When you eat a cherry, you'll turn red and will be able to touch ghosts safely and to send them back to the cage. But be careful - it will only last 10 seconds and then everything will be back to normal.
